Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to get recording consent from my meeting partner?

Yes. We strongly recommend obtaining consent before using audio translation in meetings. Here's a sample script you can use:

"For today's meeting, I'd like to use a real-time translation app to ensure accurate communication. The audio data will be temporarily stored in the cloud and automatically deleted after a set period. If you'd prefer not to, I'll switch to taking notes instead."
How accurate is the translation for technical terms?

LingoFlow uses state-of-the-art AI models for high accuracy. For specialized terminology, you can upload a custom dictionary via CSV to ensure industry-specific terms are translated correctly.

Does it work in noisy environments?

It works well in typical meeting rooms and cafes. For best results, place your smartphone close to the speaker. Extremely noisy environments may reduce accuracy.
